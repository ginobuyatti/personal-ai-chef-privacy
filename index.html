WeCook – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

WeCook ("we", "us", "our") is a meal planning app for couples and individuals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using WeCook, you agree to this policy.

Age restriction: WeCook is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email address, profile photo (via Google Sign-In)
  • App usage data: Recipes viewed, cooked, liked or disliked; meal history; macro tracking entries
  • Dietary preferences: Dietary restrictions, allergies, preferred ingredients, typical cooking time
  • Couple/pair data: When you link with a partner, your proposals, votes, and agreed recipes are visible to your partner
  • Subscription status: Active/cancelled subscription state (processed via Apple/Google; status stored in our database)
  • Local device data: Preferences, inventory, and meal history are also stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage
  • Push notification tokens: Device tokens used to send meal reminders and couple activity notifications

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized daily recipe recommendations
  • To enable the couple mode (proposals, votes, agreements)
  • To track your macros and nutritional history
  • To send push notifications (daily meal reminders, partner proposals, recipe matches). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or in the app's Profile section
  • To manage your premium subscription status
  • To improve the app using aggregated, anonymized analytics

4. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Features

WeCook uses Google Gemini AI to power the following features:

  • Automatic macro calculation from recipe ingredients
  • AI Chef Assistant (conversational recipe suggestions)
  • Weekly meal plan generation
  • Photo-based food recognition and macro analysis
⚠️ Important: AI-generated content (recipes, macro estimates, meal suggestions) may not be 100% accurate. Always consult a qualified nutritionist or healthcare professional for medical dietary advice. WeCook is not a medical app.

5. Push Notifications

WeCook uses push notifications for:

  • Daily meal reminders (configurable time)
  • Partner recipe proposals
  • Recipe match alerts (when both partners agree)

You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings or in the WeCook Profile section.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase / Google Firestore – Database, authentication, and analytics. Servers located in the United States.
  • Firebase Analytics – App usage analytics (session data, screen views, events). Data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Google Gemini AI – AI-powered features. Your ingredient lists and meal queries are sent to Gemini for processing.
  • Spoonacular API – Recipe database. Searches and queries are sent to Spoonacular servers.
  • RevenueCat – Subscription management. Processes your subscription status (not payment details — those are handled by Apple/Google).
  • Resend – Transactional email delivery (welcome, renewal, billing notifications).
  • Expo / EAS – App delivery and over-the-air updates.

7. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Firebase Firestore uses Google's industry-standard encryption at rest
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth (Google Sign-In OAuth 2.0)
  • API keys are stored as secrets in our build system and are never exposed in the app bundle

8. International Data Transfers

Firebase and Google services store data on servers located primarily in the United States. If you are located outside the US (including the European Union), your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. By using WeCook, you consent to this transfer. We rely on Google's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for GDPR compliance.

9. Couple / Partner Data Sharing

When you link with a partner in WeCook:

  • Your recipe proposals and votes are visible to your partner
  • Your agreed recipes and daily plans are shared
  • Your name and profile photo are visible to your partner
  • Your macro data and statistics are not shared with your partner

You can unlink from your partner at any time in the Couple section of the app.

10. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data (processed within 30 days)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of analytics tracking
  • California residents (CCPA): We do not sell your personal information to third parties

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@we-cook.app

11. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This will permanently delete:

  • Your profile and authentication credentials
  • All meal history, recipes, and macro data
  • Your couple connection and shared data

Data deletion is processed within 30 days. Subscription cancellation must be done separately through the App Store or Google Play.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

📧 hello@we-cook.app

WeCook – Política de Privacidad

Última actualización: 2 de mayo de 2026

1. Introducción

WeCook ("nosotros", "nos", "nuestro") es una app de planificación de comidas para parejas e individuos. Esta Política de Privacidad explica cómo recopilamos, usamos y protegemos tu información personal. Al usar WeCook, aceptás esta política.

Restricción de edad: WeCook no está dirigida a menores de 13 años. No recopilamos conscientemente datos personales de menores de 13 años.

2. Datos que recopilamos

  • Datos de cuenta: Nombre, correo electrónico, foto de perfil (vía Google Sign-In)
  • Datos de uso: Recetas vistas, cocinadas, likeadas o rechazadas; historial de comidas; registros de macros
  • Preferencias dietéticas: Restricciones, alergias, ingredientes preferidos, tiempo de cocción habitual
  • Datos de pareja: Propuestas, votos y recetas acordadas visibles para tu pareja
  • Estado de suscripción: Estado activo/cancelado (procesado por Apple/Google)
  • Datos locales: Preferencias e historial almacenados en tu dispositivo via AsyncStorage
  • Tokens de notificación: Tokens de dispositivo para enviar recordatorios y notificaciones de pareja

3. Cómo usamos tus datos

  • Para ofrecer recomendaciones de recetas personalizadas
  • Para habilitar el modo pareja (propuestas, votos, acuerdos)
  • Para rastrear tus macros e historial nutricional
  • Para enviar notificaciones push. Podés desactivarlas en cualquier momento

  • Para mejorar la app con analíticas anónimas y agregadas

4. Inteligencia Artificial (IA)

WeCook usa Google Gemini AI para:

  • Cálculo automático de macros desde ingredientes
  • Asistente AI Chef (sugerencias de recetas conversacionales)
  • Generación de plan de comidas semanal
  • Reconocimiento de alimentos por foto
⚠️ Importante: El contenido generado por IA puede no ser 100% preciso. Consultá siempre a un profesional de la salud para consejo dietético médico. WeCook no es una app médica.

5. Servicios de terceros

  • Firebase / Google Firestore – Base de datos y autenticación
  • Google Gemini AI – Funciones de IA
  • Spoonacular API – Base de datos de recetas
  • RevenueCat – Gestión de suscripciones
  • Resend – Envío de emails transaccionales
  • Expo / EAS – Entrega de la app y actualizaciones

6. Seguridad

  • Todos los datos en tránsito están cifrados con TLS/HTTPS
  • Firebase usa cifrado estándar de Google en reposo
  • Autenticación via Firebase Auth (Google Sign-In OAuth 2.0)
  • Las claves API se almacenan como secrets y nunca se exponen en el bundle

7. Tus derechos (GDPR y CCPA)

  • Acceso: Solicitá una copia de tus datos
  • Corrección: Solicitá la corrección de datos incorrectos
  • Eliminación: Eliminá tu cuenta y todos los datos asociados (procesado en 30 días)
  • Portabilidad: Solicitá tus datos en formato portable
  • Residentes de California (CCPA): No vendemos tu información personal a terceros

Para ejercer estos derechos, contactanos en hello@we-cook.app

8. Eliminación de cuenta

Podés eliminar tu cuenta desde Perfil → Zona de peligro → Eliminar cuenta. Esto eliminará permanentemente todos tus datos en 30 días.

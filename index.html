Privacy Policy / Política de Privacidad

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Personal AI Chef ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Google account name and email (when you sign in with Google)
  • Anonymous user ID (if you skip sign-in)

Profile & Preferences

  • Dietary restrictions and allergies
  • Liked and disliked ingredients
  • Preferred cooking time
  • Language and theme preferences

App Usage Data

  • Saved, cooked, and liked recipes
  • Nutritional tracking (meals, calories, macros)
  • Inventory items
  • Couple/pair connections and recipe proposals

Photos (only when you use the feature)

  • Meal photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for nutritional analysis
  • Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized recipe recommendations
  • To track your nutritional intake and goals
  • To enable couple features (shared recipes, voting)
  • To send notification reminders (only if you enable them)
  • To process in-app purchases via RevenueCat

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database (Firestore), hosting. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Gemini AI — Meal photo analysis for macros. Gemini Terms
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy
  • Expo — Push notifications. Expo Privacy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) servers. Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Some preferences (language, theme) are also stored locally on your device.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account — Available in Profile → Delete Account
  • Export your data — Contact us at the email below
  • Opt out of notifications — Manage in Profile → Notifications
  • Revoke Google access — Via your Google Account settings

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:

Email: ginobuyatti@gmail.com
